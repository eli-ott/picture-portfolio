Who am I ?

Hi, I'm Eliott. I'm a film photographer for years now. I've started photography when I retrieved my grandfather's camera.
Since then I got more and more into photography, learned about angles, lightning and more. Now I have 3 camera and all serves its own purpose.
I personnaly like architectures and art, and I think it sometimes helps me to get the perfect shot.
I also like experimenting with my pictures, trying new things that could be unconventional.

Why photography ?

The thing that I love about photography is how easy it is to interpret the same pictures in multiple ways. Everyone will have its own interpretation depending on its life and experiences.
Moreover, I like to see the smallest things in life that makes it beautiful. And with photography I can focus on them and make them big things with a huge importance.
I think photography helps me showing how I view the earth and the vision I have of it.
